Privacy Policy

At Pokefinder Online, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Your name and contact details when you create an account.
  • Information you provide when interacting with our services, such as card listings, messages, and feedback.
  • Usage information, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and track usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, including processing transactions and managing your account.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and offer tailored content and recommendations.
  • To analyze usage patterns, conduct research, and improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing services.
  • In response to a legal request or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.
  • In connection with a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the following choices and rights regarding your personal information:

  • You can update and manage your account information by logging into your account settings.
  • You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions provided in the email.
  • You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable laws.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised date will be indicated at the top of the page.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.
