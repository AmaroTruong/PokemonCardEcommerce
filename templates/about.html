About Us

Welcome to our Pokémon Card Catalogue! We are passionate about Pokémon and strive to provide a comprehensive collection of Pokémon cards for collectors and enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your journey, our catalogue offers a wide range of cards to explore and enjoy.

At Pokefinder Online, we value our loyal customers and aim to create a personalized experience for each individual. By logging in to your account, you can access additional features, manage your collection, and stay up-to-date with the latest card releases.

Browse and Sort

Our catalogue features a diverse selection of Pokémon cards, including cards from various series and generations. You can easily navigate through our collection and find your favorite cards using our sorting options. Simply select your preferred sorting criteria from the "Sort By" dropdown menu, and the cards will be arranged accordingly.

  • Alphabetically: Arrange the cards in alphabetical order based on their names.
  • Price (Lowest to Highest): Sort the cards by price, starting from the lowest.
  • Pokedex #: Organize the cards based on their Pokedex numbers.

Explore the Collection

Once you've selected your sorting preference, dive into our collection and discover the captivating world of Pokémon cards. Each card holds its own unique charm and rarity. Click on any card to explore its details, including its name, image, series, Pokedex number, and market value.
